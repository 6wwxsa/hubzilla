RedMatrix
Dream it. Do it.
The RedMatrix (aka "red") is an open source webapp providing a complete decentralised publishing, sharing, and communications system. It provides communications (private messaging, chat and social networking), media management (photos, events, files, web pages) and enough other features to make your head spin.

What makes the RedMatrix unique is the built-in decentralised identity management. This ties all RedMatrix sites and channels together into a single super-network where the boundaries between individual websites are blurred or seemingly non-existent. Other sites "magically" know you and can provide customised content and services based on who you are and what they allow you to see or do.

For the average person, this means that you decide who you want to share stuff with, and if they aren't on your list, they're not going to see it. It's all under your control (we're big on privacy). Use the RedMatrix as a social network or a business website or for personal cloud storage or media publishing - or any number of other uses limited only by your imagination.
